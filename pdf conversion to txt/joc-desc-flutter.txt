Senior Flutter Developer – Cross-Platform Mobile Applications

BrightApps Labs | Remote or Austin, TX | Full-time

COMPANY OVERVIEW

BrightApps Labs is a fast-growing product studio specializing in high-performance, cross-platform mobile applications. We partner with startups and Fortune 500 brands to deliver beautiful, scalable apps using Flutter, Firebase, and modern backend technologies.

JOB DESCRIPTION

We are seeking a Senior Flutter Developer with deep experience in building cross-platform apps from concept to deployment. The ideal candidate has a track record of shipping polished, high-performance mobile apps and mentoring junior developers in Flutter best practices.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

• Architect, build, and maintain Flutter apps for iOS and Android from a single codebase
• Integrate RESTful and GraphQL APIs, authentication, and cloud services (Firebase, AWS Amplify, Supabase)
• Implement clean UI/UX designs with responsive layouts, animations, and custom widgets
• Optimize app performance, memory usage, and startup times across devices
• Configure CI/CD pipelines for automated testing, builds, and store deployments
• Collaborate with backend teams on data modeling, caching strategies, and offline-first capabilities
• Lead code reviews, ensure adherence to best practices, and mentor junior developers
• Monitor and troubleshoot issues in production using crash analytics and logging tools
• Contribute to architectural decisions and share Flutter expertise across the company

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

• 4+ years of professional mobile development experience, including 2+ years with Flutter/Dart
• Strong knowledge of Flutter widgets, state management (Provider, Riverpod, Bloc), and navigation
• Experience integrating APIs, OAuth2 authentication, and push notifications
• Familiarity with Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions) or similar backend services
• Solid understanding of mobile performance optimization and debugging
• Proficiency in Git workflows and collaborative development
• Excellent communication and problem-solving skills

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

• Experience publishing apps to the Apple App Store and Google Play Store
• Knowledge of native Android (Kotlin) or iOS (Swift) for custom platform-specific integrations
• Familiarity with testing frameworks (Flutter Test, Mockito) and automated UI testing
• Exposure to micro-frontend or modular app architectures
• Understanding of DevOps practices and CI/CD tools (GitHub Actions, Bitrise, Codemagic)
• UI/UX sensibility and experience working with designers
• Prior startup or agency experience delivering multiple client projects in parallel
